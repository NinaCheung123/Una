# Una – Discover the Creature 🐾

Hey, I'm Una—a warm little purple creature that helps you understand yourself better.

This isn't just a journal app. It's like having a gentle friend who listens to your voice notes, organizes them, and helps you dig into why you do what you do—no judgment, no pressure.

### What Una Does Right Now

1. **Instant Spark Capture** — Tap to record your thoughts anytime (walking, showering, after a call). Keeps the original audio + auto-transcribes to text.
2. **End-of-Day Organizer** — Gentle evening nudge to create a clean daily note with your voice, summary, and light insights.
3. **Deep Creature Discovery** — Pick a note, list possible reasons (even low-probability ones), cross out what doesn't fit, keep 1-3 key drivers, and see how your understanding evolves over time.
4. **Settings to Tweak** — Adjust evening reminder time and encouragement phrases.

Everything is 100% local on your device—no cloud, no guilt.

### How to Run on Your iPhone (No Coding Skills Needed)

1. Click the green "Code" button above → Download ZIP.
2. Unzip the folder.
3. Double-click `Una.xcodeproj` to open in Xcode (free from Mac App Store).
4. Connect your iPhone to Mac with a cable (first time only).
5. In Xcode, select your iPhone from the top dropdown.
6. Click the play button ▶️ to run.

First time? It'll ask for your Apple ID (use the one on your phone—safe and free). Done!
